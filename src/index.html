Joy List

The Joylist is for people taking the 90 Days of Play Challenge. You can use this app to build your list of things that make you feel joyful. If you run out of ideas consider the following prompts:
  • What have you always dreamed of doing?
  • What is on your bucket list?
  • What do you like doing with others?
  • What would your inner 5 year old want to do?
  • What makes you go weeeeeee!?
  • What activities involve each of your five senses?
  • What makes you feel totally free?
  • What makes you feel close to God?
  • What makes you feel sexy?
  • What makes feels decadent?
  • What makes time fly by?
  • What makes you laugh?
  • What service do you enjoy doing for other people?
